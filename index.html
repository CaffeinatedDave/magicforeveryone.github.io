WHO WE ARE

Mr Crackers first began entertaining children with magic shows around 20 years ago after turning his interest in illusions into his profession. Fast forward to today and he now also offers discos with games for nursery and primary school aged children and balloon twisting too.

As a professional fun full time entertainer with over 20 years experience, he comes with full public liability, is a member of Leamington and Warwick Magic Society, Equity and has a current personal DBS check.

Get in touch to find out about our package deals, including children's games, face painting, balloon modelling, and table magic, all of which can be tailored to suit your individual needs.

WHAT WE OFFER

Children's Parties

ONE HOUR PACKAGE

The children are entertained with a interactive magic show staring Albert the naughty monkey puppet. For the grand finale, the birthday child will magically appear their very own birthday gift from Mr Crackers. The children are all given a balloon model to take home with them.

DISCO GAMES AND MAGIC SHOW

The party starts with our award winning disco with games. The games are all 'no out' games so no children are left upset. All the children win sweets, some will win toys and other prices, all provided at no extra charge.

While you then feed the children, Mr Crackers sets up his magic show. During the magic show the children meet Albert, the naughty monkey puppet, they are amazed with fun and interactive magic tricks and the birthday child magically appears their own birthday gift.

We can offer different packages to meet the needs of your party or event, just contract us with details and we will be happy to help.

DISCO, GAMES, MAGIC, BALLOON MODELLING AND FACE PAINTING

This package includes our fun filled children’s motivational disco, with games, sweets and prizes. The children will then be treated to our magic show with the birthday child magically producing their own present into an empty box. The face painter will paint throughout the party and all the children will go home with a balloon model. Please contact us for our special offer package price.

Face Painting

FACE PAINTING AND GLITTER TATTOOS

All of our face painters carry full public liability insurance and a current personal DBS. Our face painters regularly attend courses to further develop their skills and to remain up to date with changes and new products within the face painting world.

FACE PAINTING

We offer a selection of faces, such as Spiderman, Batman, pirates, Dracula, butterflies, princesses, flower girls, tigers, fantasy tigers, lions, cats, dogs and many more.

We can also paint on the arm, back of the hand or just the cheek for those youngsters who do not want at full face.

Face painting can be an optional extra with our magic and balloon shows, or our disco, games and magic shows. We also provide face painting on its own.

We use FDA compliant face paints and cosmetic grade glitter. All of our products are dated so are always within their safe usage time.

To see more photos of our face painting please visit our gallery
